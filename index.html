Our story

About Marietta Tamil School

Marietta Tamil School preserves Tamil language, culture, and identity while nurturing future generations through education, celebration, and shared experiences.

Mission

To provide high-quality Tamil language education and cultural experiences that inspire children to develop a lifelong connection to their heritage.

Vision

A thriving community that preserves Tamil language and culture for future generations while fostering pride, belonging, and lifelong learning.

Promise

We welcome every family. No child is turned away, and every parent finds a place to belong and contribute.
